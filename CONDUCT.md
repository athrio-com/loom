# Code of Conduct

We ask one thing of everyone who takes part — in issues, pull requests, and
any other project space: behave as you would want others to behave toward you.

In practice: disagree about the work, not the person; extend patience to those
still learning; assume good intent until you have clear reason not to.
Maintainers make the final call on what gets accepted — that is how it works.

Harassment, personal attacks, threats, demeaning remarks, and sharing someone's
private information without permission have no place here. Anyone asked to stop
is expected to stop.

If something crosses these lines, write to **yegor@qda.one**. Reports are
handled in confidence.
