Chapter 2

The classifier stage

WeftClassifier labels each line with its weft kind by mode. It is a Mealy machine: the state is the previous weft and whether a heading has been seen, the input a pattern probe of the line, and the output weft also feeds the next state. The marks it emits are partial — the leading marker filled, the rest left for the ::[The tokeniser stage] to settle.
